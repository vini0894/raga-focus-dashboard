_Data source: VidIQ Chrome extension + YouTube search_

## How to read this data

- **Volume (0-100):** How many people type this EXACT phrase. Higher = more search demand.
- **Competition (Low/Med/High):** How hard to rank. Lower = easier to rank.
- **Overall Score (0-100):** VidIQ's blended score. HIGH label = worth pursuing.
- **Avg Views:** How many views videos in this TOPIC get (via all discovery). Bigger = more total opportunity.

**Decision rule:** Use HIGH-score exact-match keywords in TITLE. Use 0-volume-but-high-avg-views phrases in TAGS/DESCRIPTION.

## Template: how to validate a new keyword in 30 seconds

1. Open YouTube, type the keyword in search
2. Look at VidIQ panel on right:
   - **Overall score:** must be HIGH (≥60) to prioritize
   - **Volume:** must be ≥40 for title use
   - **Competition:** Low/Medium preferred
   - **Top Creator:** if a SMALL channel is winning (<100K subs), it's a realistic target
3. Look at actual search results:
   - What title formula is the top video using?
   - What's the video length?
   - How old is the video (newer = trending keyword)?
4. Record findings in this file
